General Instructions

Welcome

You are about to participate in a study on group decision-making. Please read these instructions carefully. Your payment will depend on the decisions you and others make during the session. If you have any questions, please raise your hand. An experimenter will come to assist you privately.

General Rules
  • No talking or communicating with other participants.
  • Turn off all personal electronic devices.
  • All decisions are anonymous and confidential. You will not learn the real identity of others, nor will they learn yours.
Payment
  • You receive a $5 participation fee.
  • You can earn additional money based on decisions in the experiment, calculated in tokens.
  • Conversion: 10 tokens = $1.
  • At the end, one round will be randomly chosen for actual payment. You will be paid in cash privately.
Session Structure
  • The session consists of 10 rounds.
  • You are in a fixed group of 5 people for all rounds.
  • Each round, one person is the Incumbent; the others are Voters. The role of Incumbent rotates by election.
Round Procedure
  1. Start: You see your Personal Account balance for the round.
  2. Incumbent’s Task: The Incumbent decides how to allocate the group budget.
  3. Public Information: All see a public signal (Low, Medium, or High) indicating the group project outcome, which is related to the Incumbent’s allocation to the group project. If the current Incumbent allocates more money to public goods, it is more likely to see higher public signals.
  4. Election for Next Round: Non-Incumbents may choose to run for the next round by spending their campaign effort. Spending effort positively increases the chances of getting elected as next Incumbent. All then vote for the next Incumbent.
  5. Earnings Calculated: Your tokens for the round are updated based on all actions.
